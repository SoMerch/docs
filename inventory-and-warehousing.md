# Inventory and Warehousing

SoMerch supports managed inventory and warehousing for companies that reorder, store, or ship merch across multiple locations.

---

## Why inventory visibility matters

Distributed companies often need to know:

- what is in stock
- what is reserved
- what is running low
- what is incoming
- what can be shipped now
- what is stored for future campaigns

This helps teams avoid unnecessary reorders, delays, and manual stock checks.

---

## Common inventory workflows

SoMerch inventory workflows may include:

- SKU-level stock tracking
- batch tracking
- reserved stock
- incoming stock
- low-stock alerts
- kitting stock
- warehouse movements
- cycle counts
- returns processing

---

## Warehousing use cases

Warehousing is useful for:

- onboarding programs
- seasonal gifting
- event stock
- office restocking
- long-term brand inventory
- multi-country fulfillment
- agency-managed client programs

---

## Inventory visibility

SoMerch aims to give customers visibility into:

- available quantity
- reserved quantity
- incoming quantity
- warehouse location
- stock status
- fulfillment readiness
