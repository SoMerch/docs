Inventory and Warehousing

SoMerch supports managed inventory and warehousing for companies that reorder, store, or ship merch across multiple locations.

Why inventory visibility matters

Distributed companies often need to know what is in stock, what is reserved, what is running low, what is incoming, what can be shipped now, and what is stored for future campaigns.

Common inventory workflows

  • SKU-level stock tracking
  • batch tracking
  • reserved stock
  • incoming stock
  • low-stock alerts
  • kitting stock
  • warehouse movements
  • cycle counts
  • returns processing

Warehousing use cases

  • onboarding programs
  • seasonal gifting
  • event stock
  • office restocking
  • long-term brand inventory
  • multi-country fulfillment
  • agency-managed client programs
