Order Lifecycle

This page explains the typical lifecycle of a SoMerch order.

The exact workflow may vary depending on product type, branding method, stock availability, customer approvals, and shipping requirements.

Typical order stages

StageDescription
DraftThe order idea or request is being prepared.
Quote RequestedSoMerch reviews the request and prepares pricing and feasibility details.
QuotedThe customer receives pricing, production, and delivery information.
Approval RequiredThe order needs internal customer approval before moving forward.
ApprovedThe order has been approved.
In ProductionBranding or production has started.
Quality CheckProducts are reviewed before fulfillment or storage.
Ready for FulfillmentItems are ready to be packed, stored, or shipped.
FulfilledThe order has been completed.
On HoldThe order is paused due to missing information, stock, approval, or payment.

What can affect the order timeline

  • product availability
  • branding method
  • artwork readiness
  • sample approval
  • order quantity
  • warehouse intake
  • packaging requirements
  • number of delivery addresses
  • destination countries
  • carrier capacity
  • seasonal demand
