# Employee Gifting

SoMerch helps companies manage employee gifting and onboarding merch for distributed teams.

---

## Common employee gifting use cases

Employee gifting workflows may include:

- new hire onboarding packs
- remote employee welcome kits
- anniversary gifts
- team celebration gifts
- seasonal campaigns
- event attendee kits
- internal culture campaigns

---

## Why employee gifting is operationally difficult

Employee gifting becomes complex when teams need to manage:

- different countries
- many addresses
- apparel sizes
- budget limits
- approval steps
- stock availability
- packaging
- delivery tracking
- privacy-sensitive recipient data

---

## SoMerch workflow

A typical employee gifting workflow may include:

1. Customer selects products or a kit
2. SoMerch prepares quote and production plan
3. Customer approves the campaign
4. Stock is produced or reserved
5. Employees provide shipping details or receive assigned shipments
6. SoMerch handles pick, pack, and ship
7. Customer tracks fulfillment and delivery status
