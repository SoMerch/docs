# Approvals

Approval workflows help companies manage budgets, stakeholders, and procurement requirements before merch orders move into production or fulfillment.

---

## Why approvals matter

Corporate merch often involves several teams:

- HR
- marketing
- procurement
- finance
- operations
- office management
- employer branding

Approvals help keep decisions clear and traceable.

---

## Common approval use cases

Approvals may be used for:

- budget confirmation
- campaign owner approval
- brand review
- procurement review
- quantity approval
- delivery plan approval
- artwork approval

---

## Approval information

A SoMerch approval workflow may include:

- order reference
- quote details
- budget tag
- approver
- approval status
- internal comments
- requested changes
- approval history

---

## Typical approval statuses

| Status | Description |
|---|---|
| Pending | Waiting for a decision. |
| Approved | The order can continue. |
| Rejected | The order cannot continue in its current form. |
| Changes Requested | Updates are needed before approval. |
| Cancelled | The approval request is no longer active. |
