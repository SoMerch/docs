# How SoMerch Works

SoMerch connects corporate merch planning, production, storage, and fulfillment into one managed workflow.

The goal is to reduce the operational work required to manage branded items across distributed teams, multiple offices, and multi-country shipments.

---

## End-to-end workflow

### 1. Idea and use case

A customer starts with a use case such as:

- onboarding pack
- event kit
- employee gift
- office restock
- partner campaign
- internal brand campaign

---

### 2. Product selection

SoMerch helps the customer choose from a curated catalog of practical and tested corporate merch items.

Product selection may consider:

- target audience
- budget
- brand fit
- durability
- decoration method
- lead time
- shipping destination
- sustainability requirements

---

### 3. Quote and planning

The customer requests a quote based on:

- products
- quantities
- branding
- delivery addresses
- timeline
- packaging
- storage needs
- fulfillment requirements

---

### 4. Approval

Depending on the customer workflow, an order may require internal approval before production or fulfillment begins.

Approval may include:

- budget review
- stakeholder approval
- brand approval
- procurement review
- campaign owner approval

---

### 5. Production and quality control

After approval, products move into production.

This may include:

- artwork preparation
- print setup
- embroidery setup
- decoration
- quality control
- photo proofing
- packaging preparation

---

### 6. Warehousing or direct shipping

Depending on the order, products may be:

- shipped directly
- stored in the SoMerch warehouse
- reserved for future campaigns
- prepared as kits
- split across multiple shipments

---

### 7. Fulfillment and delivery

SoMerch prepares shipments for employees, offices, events, or partners.

Fulfillment may include:

- pick and pack
- kitting
- address validation
- carrier handoff
- shipment tracking
- issue handling
- returns support

---

## Summary

SoMerch gives distributed companies one place to manage corporate merch from idea to delivery.
