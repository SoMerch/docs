Employee Gifting

SoMerch helps companies manage employee gifting and onboarding merch for distributed teams.

Common employee gifting use cases

  • new hire onboarding packs
  • remote employee welcome kits
  • anniversary gifts
  • team celebration gifts
  • seasonal campaigns
  • event attendee kits
  • internal culture campaigns

Why employee gifting is operationally difficult

Employee gifting becomes complex when teams need to manage different countries, many addresses, apparel sizes, budget limits, approval steps, stock availability, packaging, delivery tracking, and privacy-sensitive recipient data.

Future employee flows

  • redeem links
  • employee self-serve claim pages
  • size selection
  • address collection
  • office-based shipment rules
  • team-specific gifting campaigns
  • automated reminders
