# Fulfillment and Shipping

SoMerch helps companies ship branded merch to employees, offices, events, partners, and customers across Europe.

---

## Fulfillment workflow

A typical fulfillment workflow includes:

1. Stock confirmation
2. Pick list preparation
3. Packing or kitting
4. Address validation
5. Shipping label creation
6. Carrier handoff
7. Shipment tracking
8. Delivery confirmation
9. Issue handling if needed

---

## Supported shipment types

SoMerch can support different shipment types, including:

- single office delivery
- multi-address employee shipping
- event delivery
- partner gifting
- customer gifting
- batch shipments
- stored inventory fulfillment
- onboarding pack fulfillment

---

## Multi-address shipping

Multi-address shipping is useful when companies need to send merch to many recipients.

Typical use cases include:

- remote employee onboarding
- company-wide gifting campaigns
- distributed office restocking
- team-specific campaigns
- event kits for attendees or speakers

---

## Shipping visibility

SoMerch aims to provide visibility into:

- fulfillment status
- shipment status
- carrier tracking
- delivery progress
- returned shipments
- failed delivery issues

---

## Returns and issues

Shipment issues may include:

- incorrect address
- failed delivery
- damaged parcel
- returned shipment
- missing item
- wrong size
- production defect

SoMerch handles these issues based on the order type, customer agreement, and fulfillment policy.
