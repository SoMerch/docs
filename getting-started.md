# Getting Started with SoMerch

SoMerch helps companies manage corporate merch from idea to delivery.

Instead of coordinating multiple vendors, spreadsheets, warehouses, and shipping providers, teams can use SoMerch as one managed system for corporate merch operations.

---

## What SoMerch is used for

Common use cases include:

- employee onboarding packs
- remote team gifting
- office restocking
- event and conference kits
- partner gifting
- customer campaigns
- internal brand campaigns
- managed inventory programs

---

## Typical customer teams

SoMerch is commonly used by:

- HR teams
- operations teams
- office managers
- marketing teams
- procurement teams
- employer branding teams
- agencies and studios

---

## Basic workflow

A typical SoMerch workflow looks like this:

1. Choose a use case
2. Select products from the curated catalog
3. Request a quote
4. Review product, branding, and delivery details
5. Approve the order
6. Move to production
7. Store stock or ship directly
8. Track fulfillment and delivery

---

## What makes SoMerch different

SoMerch combines service and software:

- curated product selection
- in-house production and quality control
- warehousing and inventory visibility
- multi-address shipping
- approval workflows
- order and shipment tracking
- EU-focused fulfillment
